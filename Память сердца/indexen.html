1941-1945

A witness to the events of the Great Patriotic War, a resident of Kiev Nina Gerasimova, left behind a diary with memories, which is now available on our website.
When reading the diary, you will have the opportunity to mentally find yourself in June 1941, during the graduation period, when children just finished school got to the front,without feeling their childhood at all...
You will learn from the mouth of kieyavnka Nina how the war destroyed millions of innocent lives,how the war swept away everything in its path, sparing no one and nothing. And how in one moment life was divided into before and after...
Nina Nikolaevna Gerasimova is a member of the underground Komsomol anti-fascist organization "Young Guard".
